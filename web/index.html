Verification

Pick a bundle. The verifier uses only public chain data — no node, no keys, no trust in the prover — and re-derives every spot in the transaction where a payment to the address could exist.
    One primitive, four sentences: I was paid / it paid me nothing (receiver) · I paid you / I did not pay that (sender). Built on bdk-sp — Monero's InProof/OutProof, ported to Bitcoin.
